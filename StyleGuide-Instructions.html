Typographic Style

There are some typography examples for U.S Department of Education agency. Since the agency is for multiple types of users there are not so strong typographies only for one type of user. This is an education agency so students will use the web site, the parents will use, grants will use.. I have chosen typography which is clear for everyone, easy to understand, minimalist, modern but not cold. Friendly, warm, classic and not boring, modern typography. Cardo, Lato and Libre Franklin font styles are used.
